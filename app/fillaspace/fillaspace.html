Fill-A-Space

Visual Identity
Fill-A-Space is a boutique furniture servicing company serving the Chicagoland area and surrounding suburbs, offering a wide selection of residential, office & special event furnishings for temporary & permanent accommodations.

I created the boutique furniture servicing company’s identity system as my first project at Fill-A-Space. The owner of the company wanted to have a logo that speaks about what the company does and also applies to all the various marketing pieces.

I started with sketching floor plan and simplifying it. I found out I could use initial letter F, A, and S to make a square, which is also similar to a floor plan. In addition, navy blue represents trust, and grass green shows the company is young and full of energy. Every element of the logo together indicates the core of the company’s spirit: Mood, Sketch, and Stage.
